JEE Advanced

  • JEE Advanced is the examination taken for admission in IIT.
  • JEE Advanced is conducted by one of the seven zonal IITs Roorkey,Kharagpur,Delhi,Kanpur,Bombay,Madras,Guwahai.
  • This year JEE advanced will be conducted by IIT Kharagpur.
  • In total 2,50,000 students will be appearing in JEE Advanced.

IIT

  • IIT stands for Indian Institute of Technogy is an autonomous university located across India.
  • There are 23 IITs all across the country and all these IITs are regulated by IIT council.
  • The IIT Delhi has world wide ranking of 172.
  • The IIT has Courses like B.Sc, B.Tech, MTech, BS & MS, B.Arch, B.Des, Ph.D, M.Phil, joint in MSc-PhD.
  • As of 2020 the total number of seats in IIT is 16,0532
  • IITs are self autonomous universities and they have right to change their courses as per requirements.
  • B.Tech are the most commom undergraduate education in IITs.
  • Enrolement in IITs is considered to be one of the most difficult admission.
  • IITs also provide an unconventional dual degree in both bachelore and master for 5 years.
  • Many IITs have global ranking below 200 acoss the world.

    Steps to fill JEE advanced application form

    Threr are four steps in filling up of JEE advanced application form.

    • Registration of JEE Advanced
    • Uploading documents
    • Payment of registraton fees
    • Conformation pages

    Registration of JEE advanced

    1. logon to website provided in the link here or visit the official wbsite.
    2. Read all the instructions carefully and check whether you fall under the eligibility criteria or not.
    3. You can log on to the officcal website through roll number, password and security pin.
      Foreign nationals are to remember that they will have a seperate registration potal and they can register for JEE Advaanced without attending JEE mains.
    4. Candidates will be displayed with their identity in the page as filled in the JEE main application form.
      Foregn nationals registering directly to JEE mains should enter the required fields like academic, personal and contact details.

    Uploading documents

    1. Upload the documents in the required format.
