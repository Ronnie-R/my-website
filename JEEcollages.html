Engeneering collages in India.

Yoou can expect two types of engeneering institutes in India providing technical education government and private.

Government engeneering instittes are the instittes run undr the direct supervision of government of India or state government of India.These instituts includes IITs, NITs and IITs and so on.

Private engeneering institutes include all deemed universities and non-government funded universities.

How to get admission in government funded universitiies ?

The admission process in different institutes is differnt.

Admission in IIT :In order to get admisssion in IIT students should attend JEE main and JEE advanced both. They should then get admission in IIT through JoSSA seat allocation system.

Admission in NIT: In order to get admission in NIT one should attend JEE main, if the students attend a valid marks in JEE main then he/she will get admission in NIT. Marks obtained by studentts in JEE main is the determining factor for admission in NIT.

Admission in IIT: Admission in IIIT are done on the basis of marks of JEE mains, if a students have not attended JEE mains then the examination conducted by IITs themself can be considered sometimes.

Admission in CFTIs: JEE main is the only way to get admission in CFTIs. The studens get admission in CFTIs on the basis of their performance in JEE main.

How to get admission in deemed or private universities?

Before we get into detaails let us first understand the fndamentally what deemed or private universities mean.

Both deemed and private universities are nearly similar but deemed universities have a right to apply their own rules and regulation and they enjoy the autonomy of rules and regulation.

Some deemed universities accept JEE main scores to select sstudents for undergraduate courses, while many other conducts their own entrance examination which you may have to pass to get admission in it.
