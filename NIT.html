JEE mains

JEE mains is conducted twice every year ones in April and ones in January. The marks of JEE mains is determining for admission in NITs, and appearence in JEE advanced.

NIT

  1. NIT stands for National Institute of Technology.
  2. There are in total 31 NIT all across India.
  3. NIT offers admission through JEE main result and NIT offers admission for vaarious undergraduate and post graduate and Ph.D programmes.
  4. NIT also provides 5 years dual degree program for engeneering and Science courses.
