Best books for NEET examination

We need to know well how we need to study before preparing for NEET examination. Most students forget one thing they need to know the syllabus well before prepearing for examination.

So, before preparing for examination. Just know the syllabus well and know which chapter is most important and prepare the same way.

For any students it is important to know the basic concepts well and start with any easy book to do so. Without basic concepts your preparation will always be lagging behind

For students from Nepal we advise the following books for basic concepts:

Biology
  1. Biology of Sukunda publication for class 11
  2. Biology of Asmita publication class 11
  3. Biology of Vidhyarthi publication class 11
  4. Biology of Ekta publication class 11

We recommend keeping two books and reading both of them for any chapter. Personally speaking Asmita publication book is pretty good and technical so we recommend it as one of the book. Similarly Sukunda's publication book is easy to understand your second choice for it will not be a bad one.

  1. Biology of Asmita publication for class 12
  2. Biology of Vidhyarthi publication for class 12
  3. Biology of Ekta publiication for class 12

We dont recommend Sukunda's book for grade 12. It is of no use. We recommend you with book of Asmita publication as your first choice and any other book as your second choice.

Even the choice of book for IOM entrance examination is same. You can also read the books provided by NAME. It is also preety much consise and entrance exam focused but it is only sufficient if you are IOM focused for NEET. You need many other books for biology

  1. Trueman Vol 1 and Vol 2
  2. Objective biology by Dinesh
  3. Pradeep guide on Biology
  4. GRB publication for Biology
Chemistry
  1. Chemistry of Asmita publication for grade 11.
  2. Chemistry of Ekta publication for grade 11
  3. Chemistry of Dreemland publication for grade 11
  4. Chemistry of Heritage publcation for grade 11

Among these books Asmita's chemistry book is pretty good. You will love the Asmita's publication book for being short and to the point. Book of Dreamland publication is hard to understand for no reason but we think it is also a good book rather than buying book of Dreamland publication we would suggest you to use book of Heritage or Ekta pubication. The book of Ekta publication is good in a sense that it has many other information too outside the syllabus and book of Heritage publication is consise and easy to understand.

  1. Chemistry of Asmita publication for grade 12.
  2. Chemistry of Ekta publication for grade 12.
  3. Chemistry of Heritage publication for grade 12.
  4. Chemistry of Dreemland publication for Grade 12.

Among these books we suggest you to use Asmita's and Ekta's book for Grade 12. Asmita's book contain reaction mechanism explained of chemial reactions and book of Ekta publication contain a lot of chemical reactions. So, each books has its own advantages. Similarly, book of Heritage publication will explain every topic in an easy way.

Although all these books are quite good but they are not sufficient for entrance examination. Also it is impoortant to remember that you acannot ignore the informations provided by these book.Besides these book the books necessary are:

  1. GRB organic and inorganic chemistry.
  2. Disha's organic chemistry.
  3. Organic chemistry objective by Arihant publication.
  4. J.D. Lee Consise Inorganic chemistry for solid states, co-ordination bonds, chemical bonding and atomic structure.

Best books for JEE examination

JEE is all about practicing maximum number of questions after clearing the basic concepts. Just remember one thing you need to practice past questions and after that we need to practice questions outside that.

Basic books are good way to clear concepts first and then move forward.

Physics
  1. Physics of Arihant publication for Grade 11 and 12.
  2. Physics of Asmita's publication for Grade 11 and 12.
  3. SL Arora book for Physics all $ volumes

Although these books are good you can depend on SL Arora book for Physics .It is good for basic knowledge. We need many other books to cover further concepts like:

  1. DC Panday physics book.
  2. Anurag Mishra's physics book
  3. IE Irodov numerical physics

We need to practice numerical from all these books and we need to know all these concepts quite well. IE Irodov book on physics is a masterspiece but It is far more time consuming. Even Anurag Mishra's book for Physics is a good one. We can depend on it for IIT/JEE.

  1. 40 years of Chapterwise solved questions of Disha or Arihant.
Chemistry

We have a lot of books to get basic knowledge. We should read the following books for better knowledge in topics

  1. GRB physical chemistry.
  2. GRB inorganic chemsitry.
  3. GRB organic chemistry.
  4. Consise inorganic chemistry by J.D Lee
  5. Disha's organic chemstry
  6. GRB numerical chemistry.

All these books are quite importanat to have knowledge on Chemistry. We need to have through knowledgen on Chemistry with these books.

All these books are quite good and important for chemistry none of the books can be missed in addition to these books you also need to practice past questions for IIT/JEE.

  1. 40 years of Chapterwise solved questions of Disha or Arihant.
  2. MS Chauhan is the book for Organic chemistry to practice.
  3. Balagi inorganic chemistry is the chemistry book to practice.

Do practice questions in these books and solve properly to get a good knowledge in chemistry.
