NEET Exam Centers

  • NEET 2021 will be conducted in 155 test cities and across 3,834 test centers.
  • Students can choose desired examination centers from these cities while filling the aplication form
  • Candidates are required to choose the examination center carefully as they are not allowed to change the examination center ones allocated.
  • Any information about examination centers will be provided in the admit card only.
  • Students are advised to check the examinaton center atleast a day before examination.

How to know your examination center?

  • Go to the link ntanic.in
  • Enter candidates login details
  • Enter application login and password
  • The 'Advance Information' pop-up will open the screen
  • Check the details and take screenshots for future reference
